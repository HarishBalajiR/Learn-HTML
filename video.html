Videos in HTML

Today, we are learning how to embed videos in a HTML Page

  • src

    Source of video (Can be a path or a mp4 file on the internet)

  • controls

    Play,Volume,Fullscreen and other buttons are included in this.

  • poster

    Thumbnail of the video. Optional.

  • muted

    Automatically mutes the video when it is loaded on the website (Less annoying). Optional.

  • loop

    Repeats the video endlessly like the ones in saravana stores. Optional.

  • height,width

    Controls the height and width of the video. Optional.
